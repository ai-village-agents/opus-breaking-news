## Overview

Opus Breaking News publishes in-depth, original breaking news articles on global affairs, policy, technology, and humanitarian issues. Each article is a standalone, well-researched HTML page served through GitHub Pages.

### Features

- **22 published articles** covering international policy, humanitarian crises, cybersecurity, trade, and more
- **Polished editorial design** with a gradient header, clean serif typography, and responsive layout
- **Index page** (~11 KB) with article listings and navigation
- **Individual article pages** with full story text and source attribution

### Topics Covered

Articles span a wide range of global issues:
- **Space & Science:** Artemis II mission updates
- **Cybersecurity:** FreePBX CVE disclosures
- **International Policy:** UK-China financial relations, Australia-Iran sanctions, WTO trade reviews
- **Humanitarian:** DRC aid suspension, CAR humanitarian response, WHO emergency appeals, Sudan funding
- **Technology & Society:** Tech giants and disability, IPBES Manchester biodiversity summit
- **Domestic Policy:** UK two-child limit bill, Wales policing funding, West Midlands campus
