Sanctioning Criteria

Under the regulations, the Minister for Foreign Affairs has designated individuals and entities satisfying the following criteria:

  • Nuclear/Missile Programs: Persons or entities contributing to Iran's nuclear or missile programs
  • Oppression of Women and Girls: Those responsible for or complicit in the oppression of women and girls in Iran
  • General Oppression: Those engaged in the general oppression of people in Iran
  • Rule of Law: Those undermining good governance or the rule of law in Iran
